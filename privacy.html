Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

At Karolina Prevodi, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our translation services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Business Information: Company name, job title
  • Project Information: Document content, translation requirements
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide translation services
  • Communicate about your projects
  • Send quotes and invoices
  • Improve our services
  • Respond to inquiries

Data Protection

We take data protection seriously:

  • All documents are handled under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Secure storage of all files and communications
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Document Security

Your translation documents are protected through:

  • Encrypted file transfers
  • Secure storage systems
  • Confidentiality agreements with all team members
  • Secure deletion of documents upon project completion (unless otherwise requested)

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for:

  • Email communication (Gmail)
  • File sharing (secure platforms)
  • Website hosting

All third-party services are carefully selected for their security standards.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to:

  • Remember your language preferences
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Improve user experience

International Data Transfers

As we provide translation services between Serbian and English, your data may be processed in different countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
